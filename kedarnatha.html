Package Overview

Kedarkantha trek is everything a trekker can dream about; crystal clear mountain, the verdant expanse of rich flora, unexplored mountain terrain of Uttarakhand, jaw-dropping campsites, the serenity of mountain villages and a glimpse into the distinct culture of locals, the trek is a wholesome package of fun and adventure. Experiencing the local culture and feasting on local meals is another attraction of the Kedarkantha trek. The warm hospitality of locals is such a rewarding experience that it leaves you wondering at their magnanimity.

Inclusions

  • All the trek equipment will be included like a sleeping bag, kitchen & dining tent, utensils, mattress, tent, toilet tent, and crampon (if needed).
  • Microspikes and Gaitors for saftey of our trekkers.
  • All the needed entry fees along with permits.
  • First aid medical kits, oxygen cylinder as well as stretcher.
  • Meals during the trek only vegetarian.
  • Hiking skilled and proficient trek leader, cook,guide, and support staff

Exclusions

  • The food at the time of transit.
  • All kinds of personal expenditures.
  • Anything that we have not mentioned beforehand.
  • Mules or porter to carry private baggage.
  • Any kind of emergency evacuation charges.

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1

Pickup From Dehradun and drive to Sankri

  • Sankri is located at an altitude of 1920 meters and 6,400 feet.
  • Dehradun to Sankri 220km drive which will take almost 10 hours.
  • All the trekkers will be pickuped from assigned location at 7:00AM in a Bolero (6-7 seaters).
  • You will be reaching at Sankri till 05 in the evening.

Day 2

Trek from Sankri to Juda-kaTalab

  • Sankri is located at an altitude of 1920 meters and 6,400 feet
  • Dehradun to Sankri 220km drive which will take almost 10 hours.
  • All the trekkers will be pickuped from assigned location at 7:00AM in a Bolero (6-7 seaters).
  • You will be reaching at Sankri till 05 in the evening.

Day 3

Trekking from Juda-ka- Talab to Kedarkantha Base (Talkhetra)

  • Today we will trek to 11250 ft from a height of 9100 ft.
  • Trekking for 2kms which will take time of 2.5 hours.Most of the trail is on a ride and under the oak trees
  • You will be passing through some pastures where shepherd log huts can be seen.
  • Night stay in tents, giving amazing views of nature.

Day 4

Early Morning Hike From Kedarkantha base to Kedarkantha peak; descend to Sankri base

  • We Will Wake Up Early Mornig for the summit push / peak
  • Trekking for almost 10-12 hours for 12kms trek
  • You will see sun rise if we reach on time to summit
  • Served with lip-smacking lunch at the camp base only
  • After lunch, descending towards Sankri via Hargaon
  • For snacks time we will reach to Sankri base
  • Night stay will be organized in the same homestay.

Day 5

After Having Last Tea With Us, Departure from Sankri to Dehradun

  • Back to Dehradun which is 220km from Sankri.
  • The drive will take almost 10 hours but, on the way, you will come across beautiful scenes.
  • By Bolero or any other vehicle, you will be dropped at Dehradun Railway Station.

Duration: 4 Nights & 5 days

Places to Visit: 4N Kedarnatha
